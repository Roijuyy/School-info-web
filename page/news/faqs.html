Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries regarding registration timelines, academic programs, and school operations.

What is the registration timeline for new terms?

Applications remain open year-round; however, core term registrations close two weeks prior to the opening semester dates. We highly suggest submitting early via our digital entry form.

Does EduFuture offer technical or STEM specific tools?

Yes, all structural levels from primary to high school pathways participate in computational logic, robotics labs, and integrated science experiments regularly inside our campus workshops.

What are the valid payment modes accepted for tuition?

We securely accept regular bank transfers, institutional processing check cards, and online portal installments. Complete structured fees can be reviewed on our specialized Tuition page.
