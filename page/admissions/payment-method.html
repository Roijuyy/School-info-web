Payment Options

We support several pathways to settle institutional accounts securely:

  • Bank Counter Transfer: Pay via mobile app or counter slip directly into our verified corporate vault account.
  • KHQR Instant Scan: Instantly clear tuition balances at our physical school cashier counter.
  • Installment Plan: Request an automated monthly or termly bank-deducted structured split option.
